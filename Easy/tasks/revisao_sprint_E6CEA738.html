Task: Revisao da Sprint
Mostre o que foi feito e funciona. Desenhe conseqüências.
Disciplines: Easy Tasks
Purpose

O objetivo é mostrar os requisitos feitos durante o sprint com uma demonstração do produto parcial.

      Process Usage
      • Easy Lifecycle  >  Sprint phases  >  Sprint  >  Revisao da Sprint Review
      Steps
      Prepare the demo
      The meeting preparation must not exceed one hour.
      Perform the demo
      The team presents the partial product, result of its development tasks, through a demo of the implemented requirements (user stories). Only fully completed (and tested) requirement implementations (et testées) are presented. This allows to get an objective measurement of the work progress.
      Evaluate Sprint results

      The Product Owner and attending stakeholders (customers, users) ask questions to the team, give impressions, make proposals and change requests. The Product Backlog is enhanced with found bugs and enhancement requests.

      Calculate actual velocity

      The Sprint Velocity is the sum of all associated points to requirements checked as fully implemented. It is then compared to the one from previous sprints, usually through a Release Burndown Chart.

      Adjust the Release planning

      Conditions may have changed since the last release planning:

      • items may have been added, or deleted, estimations updated, 
      • item prioritization has been changed,

      in addition, average velocity may have evolved. 

      The Release planning is adjusted, taking into account these new parameters.

      Key Considerations

      Trabalho em equipe (durante a reunião de avaliação da Sprint).

      For the meeting:

      • max length:  2 hours
      • average length: 1 hour
